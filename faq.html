LomaShares is an online platform where users can register, choose an investment plan, and earn returns based on the selected package over a fixed duration.

Open the registration page, enter your correct details, and complete sign-up. After that, log in to access your dashboard and continue with deposits or investments.

Users fund their wallet through the deposit page. Once payment is successfully confirmed, the wallet balance is updated and becomes available for investment.

Go to the Invest page, select a plan, and confirm the investment with your wallet balance. The plan details will then appear inside your account.

Daily profit is processed automatically according to the platform schedule. Only eligible active investments receive daily profit for that cycle.

Every user gets a referral code and referral link. When someone signs up with your link and completes a qualifying investment, you may receive a referral reward based on the current system setup.

A gift code is a redeemable code that credits a specified amount to a user wallet, depending on the reward or campaign attached to that code.
